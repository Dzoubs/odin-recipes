Pizza

This is a great example of a pepperoni pizza!

This recipe is very simple, but it does require some patience!

Having a pizza oven isn't even required, everything can be done in your cast iron pan!

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough (homemade or store-bought)
  • ⅓ cup Pizza Sauce
  • 4 oz. Mozzarella Cheese, grated
  • 16 slices Pepperoni
  • 4 oz Fresh Mozzarella Balls, drained, halved
  • ½ small Red Onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 Sweet Peppers, seeded, thinly sliced
  • Pinch of Salt and Ground Black Pepper
  • 8 Fresh Basil Leaves, torn
  • Extra-virgin Olive Oil (for brushing dough)

Steps

  1. Roll out the dough on a lightly floured surface. Use a rolling pin to roll out the dough into a 12” diameter or as big as you want, depending on how thick you want the pizza crust.
  2. Sprinkle a tiny bit of semolina over a pizza pan or cast-iron skillet for an easy release.
  3. Place the dough on a pizza pan. The dough might retract. Oil the tip of your fingers and gently stretch the dough pulling outwards towards you. Use the tip of a fork to prick the dough slightly, careful not to poke through the dough.
  4. Brush the edge of the dough with extra virgin olive oil.
  5. Next, preheat the oven to 450°F.
  6. Assemble the pizza using the back of a spoon. Spread the pizza sauce onto the dough, leaving about an inch of crust around the edges. Add a tablespoon at a time.
  7. Add the pepperoni, top with the fresh and shredded mozzarella (pat dry any excess liquid). Add the onion and peppers. Sprinkle a pinch of salt and black pepper.
  8. Bake for 12 -15 minutes or until the crust is browned. Remove from the oven and finish it off with fresh basil leaves. Drizzle with a tiny bit of extra-virgin olive oil.
  9. Slice and serve.
